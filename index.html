Proposal for Squid Game 2 Pop-Up Shops Promotional Plan

Hey Netflix Team,

Thanks so much for considering us to bring Squid Game Season 2 to life through immersive pop-up shops. We’re excited to share a concept that blends digital innovation with iconic elements from the show to create an unforgettable fan experience.

Proposal Overview

We propose a multi-faceted campaign built around interactive and immersive elements:

Thematic Merchandise

  • Squid Game masks, tracksuits, and limited-edition collectibles.
  • Each item is embedded with NFC tags or QR codes, unlocking:
    • Exclusive trailers.
    • Behind-the-scenes content.
    • Mini-games inspired by the series’ challenges.

Interactive Installations

  • Digital ads in high-traffic areas (e.g., malls, transit stations) featuring:
    • Mini-games or quizzes modeled after Squid Game challenges.
    • Photo opportunities with iconic props, making them Instagram-worthy moments.

Key Features

  • Brand Engagement: Amplify excitement for Netflix and Squid Game through fun, shareable experiences.
  • Customer Interaction: Draw fans to pop-up shops while promoting interactive content tied to the show.
  • Revenue Growth: Increase merchandise sales and drive Netflix subscriptions through immersive fan engagement.

User Persona

Name: Alex Kim

Age: 26

Occupation: Marketing Coordinator

Location: Toronto, Canada

Motivations

  • Immersive and engaging experiences tied to their favorite shows.
  • Discovering exclusive content and limited-edition merchandise.

Pain Points

  • Overcrowded and uninspired advertising clutter.
  • Lack of meaningful interaction with promotional campaigns.

Behaviors

  • Commutes daily via public transit, often using their smartphone.
  • Regularly streams TV shows and actively participates in fan communities.

Onboarding Storyboard

  1. Introduction: Alex notices a brightly colored installation resembling Squid Game’s signature items.
  2. Engagement: They tap their phone on the NFC tag or scan a QR code on the installation.
  3. Interaction: Alex’s phone opens an interactive webpage, inviting them to play a mini “Survival Game” inspired by Squid Game, or directly watch the TV show.
  4. Rewards: Successfully completing the game unlocks access to exclusive behind-the-scenes content, discounts on merchandise, and a chance to win tickets to a themed event.
  5. Social Sharing: Alex shares their experience on social media, spreading excitement for Squid Game Season 2 among their peers.
